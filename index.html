A Master's Final Wish

Kyabje Garchen Rinpoche has expressed that his final unfulfilled wish is to establish a comprehensive online language school to preserve and transmit the linguistic heritage of the Tibetan Buddhist tradition – securing it for generations to come.

This platform will offer world-class instruction in Sanskrit, Classical Tibetan, and Colloquial Tibetan — freely accessible to anyone in the world who wishes to learn. No tuition. No barriers. Only Dharma.

What We Will Build

A comprehensive digital platform offering three pillars of Buddhist linguistic heritage — produced by exceptional instructors and freely available to every student on earth.

བོད་སྐད།

Colloquial Tibetan

A complete three-tier program — from foundational literacy to spoken fluency — enabling students worldwide to communicate in Central Tibetan dialect.

བོད་ཡིག།

Classical Tibetan

Unlocking direct access to Buddhist scripture and philosophy in the literary language refined over centuries to carry the subtleties of the Dharma.

संस्कृतम्

Sanskrit

Restoring connection to the original language of the Buddha's teachings — the root from which all Buddhist traditions flowered.

❖

Addressing a Need

While traditional language schools exist, they remain out of reach for countless students who lack the flexibility to travel or the financial means required to attend. By investing in exceptional instructors and producing high-quality video instruction, we will make authentic language education available to students everywhere.

This is not simply an educational initiative — it is an act of preservation. As Tibetan language schools throughout China face systematic closure and the next generation of Himalayan Buddhists grows increasingly distant from their linguistic heritage, the need for this platform has never been more urgent.

❖

Blessed by His Holiness the Dalai Lama

His Holiness shares the profound wish that the Tibetan language be preserved and strengthened on the global stage. Upon hearing Kyabje Garchen Rinpoche's aspiration, His Holiness blessed the vision and the project, seeing it as a fulfillment of his own mission.

"This initiative carries the blessing and vision of these great masters."
