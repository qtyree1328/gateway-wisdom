Why Now

Tibetan language schools across China are closing. A generation is losing connection to its linguistic heritage. Traditional instruction remains out of reach for students who cannot travel or afford tuition.

By investing in exceptional instructors and high-quality video production, this platform will make authentic language education freely available to every student on earth.

Be Part of This Legacy

The instructors are assembled. The curriculum is being written. The digital infrastructure is being donated. What remains is the funding to bring it all together.
